About

I am a public health graduate student and case aide who is building skills in health data analysis, policy communication, and program evaluation. My background in anthropology and social services shapes the way I approach public health data: not just as numbers, but as evidence connected to real communities, systems, and decisions.

“Give me a public health problem, a messy dataset, and a system that needs explaining. I will probably make a dashboard about it.”
